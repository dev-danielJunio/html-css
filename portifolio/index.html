Travel to Japan

Sights abound in major Japanese cities. In Tokyo, for example, take the opportunity to discover the efficient subway and icons such as the Sensoji temple, Tokyo Disneyland, Tokyo Bay and Mount Fuji, one of the main symbols of Japan. near the beautiful cherry trees. The landscape of these trees in Ueno Park has become a postcard. If shopping is your thing, explore Shibuya, an area full of shops and colorful signs, and the Ginza neighborhoods and surrounding Omotesando boulevard. Also stroll through the Tokyo National Museum and be sure to photograph the view of the city with the second tallest tower in the world in the background, the Tokyo SkyTree.
